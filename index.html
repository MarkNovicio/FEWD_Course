Background

I am a firm believer in God and His saving work on the cross for all mankinds sins. I believe Bachelors in Geography and Masters in Geographical Information Systems. While going for my Masters, I was introduced to Web Development. Since then, I have been working on improving that skill. I have created a variety of web pages for others and created many on my own. I have grown a desire for web development and would enjoy to see others gain a new skill. Would like to turn this desire for good use.

Why?

I believe there are many individuals hurting, searching in the wrong places, feeling stuck or empty in life. I believe many folks tend to fill that void with what they see here in earth. In Matthew 6 it mentions where your treasure is there your heart will be also. If your treasure is what you see, feel, taste, touch. Then your heart will reflect that. Whatever happens to what you treasure is what will happen to your heart. If your treasure is God, who never changes and loves man to the point of death on the cross, then you will be safe. HOWEVER if it is anything else, worry, anxiety, fear will overcome your heart.
